How to trust this certificate authority in the Chrome browser

  1. First, download this file to your computer
  2. Second, go to chrome settings and then to "Privacy and Security" > "Security"
  3. Third, click on "Manage Certificates"
  4. Fourth, click on the "Import" button in the window that pops up
  5. Fifth, click next, and then click on "Browse" and select the file that you downloaded in step 1
  6. Sixth, click "next" to install the certificate for Chrome
